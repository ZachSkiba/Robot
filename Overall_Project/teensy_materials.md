## ⚠️ Critical Build Rules

- **Capacitor Rule**  
  Every **TMC2209** used on a breadboard **must** have a capacitor across **VM/GND**.  
  Without it, the driver *will* fail on first deceleration.

- **Relay Rule**  
  The **E-Stop button drives the relay coil**.  
  The relay — *not the button* — carries the 15A motor current.

- **Ferrite Rule**  
  Place ferrite beads on:
  - USB cable (Teensy end)
  - 24V power lines entering each driver
